Pavel Shevchenko

is a student in computer science at Michigan State University. Born in Lansing, Michigan to immigrant parents, he has spent a majority of his life in the East Lansing/Okemos area with a few months spent in Russia and Ukraine at a young age. He enjoys spending time doing outdoor activities such as longboarding, rock climbing, and hiking as well as playing and refereeing soccer. On top of this, Pavel has been playing the violin since fifth grade.

Pavel is also the President of Sigma Chi at Michigan State University (MSU). As one of the founding fathers of the Gamma Psi chapter, he is in the process of helping Sigma Chi reestablish their presence at MSU and get back into their house. Throughout this time he has learned valuable skills and has made lifelong brothers.
